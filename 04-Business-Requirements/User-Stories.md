# User Stories

## HR Director
As an HR Director, I want to identify departments with high attrition so that I can improve employee retention strategies.

---

## CEO
As a CEO, I want to understand overall employee turnover trends so that I can make strategic workforce decisions.

---

## Finance Manager
As a Finance Manager, I want to analyze the financial impact of attrition so that recruitment and replacement costs can be reduced.

---

## Department Manager
As a Department Manager, I want to identify employees at higher attrition risk so that proactive engagement actions can be taken.

---

## HR Analyst
As an HR Analyst, I want interactive dashboard filters so that I can analyze attrition across different employee groups.
