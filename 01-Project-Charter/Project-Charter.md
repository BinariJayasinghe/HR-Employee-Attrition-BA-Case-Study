# Project Charter: HR Employee Attrition Analysis

## Project Name
HR Employee Attrition Analysis

## Objective
To identify key factors contributing to employee attrition and provide business recommendations to improve employee retention.

## Business Problem
The organization is experiencing employee turnover, which may impact productivity, recruitment cost, team stability, and employee morale.

## Project Scope
This analysis focuses on employee attrition patterns based on department, job role, income, overtime, job satisfaction, work-life balance, and years at company.

## Stakeholders
- CEO
- HR Director
- Department Managers
- Finance Manager
- Employees

## Expected Deliverables
- Business Analysis documentation
- Stakeholder analysis
- Requirement gathering document
- Business requirements document
- Process flow
- Power BI dashboard
- Insights and recommendations report

## Success Criteria
The project will be successful if it identifies major attrition drivers and provides clear, actionable recommendations for employee retention.
