# Requirement Gathering Document

## Introduction
This document outlines the business needs, stakeholder expectations, and analytical requirements for the HR Employee Attrition Analysis project.

The purpose of the project is to identify the major causes of employee attrition and provide data-driven recommendations to improve employee retention.

---

# Business Problem

The organization is facing increasing employee attrition, leading to:

- High recruitment costs
- Loss of experienced employees
- Reduced productivity
- Workforce instability
- Increased operational pressure

Management requires analytical insights to understand the reasons behind employee turnover.

---

# Requirement Gathering Techniques

The following requirement elicitation techniques were considered during this project:

| Technique | Purpose |
|-----------|---------|
| Stakeholder Interviews | Understand management concerns |
| Observation | Analyze workforce behavior patterns |
| Document Analysis | Review employee-related metrics |
| Data Analysis | Identify attrition trends and correlations |

---

# Stakeholder Requirements

## CEO
- Understand overall attrition trends
- Receive strategic recommendations
- Improve workforce stability

## HR Director
- Identify major attrition factors
- Monitor department-level turnover
- Analyze employee satisfaction

## Finance Manager
- Reduce recruitment and replacement costs
- Analyze salary-related attrition

## Department Managers
- Identify high-risk employee groups
- Improve employee engagement

---

# Analytical Requirements

The dashboard should provide:

- Attrition rate analysis
- Department-wise attrition
- Salary impact analysis
- Job satisfaction analysis
- Work-life balance impact
- Overtime analysis
- Employee demographic analysis

---

# Success Criteria

The project will be considered successful if:

- Key attrition drivers are identified
- Business insights are generated clearly
- Recommendations support decision-making
- Dashboard enables interactive analysis
