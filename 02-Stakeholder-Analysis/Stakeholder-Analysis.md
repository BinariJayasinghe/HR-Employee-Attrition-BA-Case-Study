# Stakeholder Analysis

## Purpose
This document identifies the key stakeholders involved in the HR Employee Attrition Analysis project and analyzes their level of interest and influence.

| Stakeholder | Role | Interest Level | Influence Level | Key Expectations |
|-------------|------|----------------|------------------|------------------|
| CEO | Executive Sponsor | High | High | Reduce attrition and improve workforce stability |
| HR Director | HR Operations Lead | High | High | Identify causes of employee turnover |
| Department Managers | Department Supervisors | High | Medium | Understand department-level attrition trends |
| Finance Manager | Financial Oversight | Medium | High | Reduce recruitment and replacement costs |
| Employees | Workforce | Medium | Low | Better work-life balance and job satisfaction |

---

## Stakeholder Communication Strategy

| Stakeholder | Communication Method | Frequency |
|-------------|----------------------|------------|
| CEO | Executive summary reports | Monthly |
| HR Director | Dashboard reviews and meetings | Weekly |
| Department Managers | Department insights reports | Bi-weekly |
| Finance Manager | Cost impact analysis reports | Monthly |

---

## Key Stakeholder Concerns

- Increasing employee turnover rates
- Employee dissatisfaction
- Overtime workload impact
- Low employee retention in specific departments
- Cost associated with employee replacement
